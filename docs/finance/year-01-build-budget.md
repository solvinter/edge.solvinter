# Accra Node 0.1 – Year One Budget

Status: Draft

## Purpose

The purpose of Node 0.1 is not to maximize profit.

Its purpose is to demonstrate that a small, quiet, well-designed workspace in central Accra can finance a broader community node focused on learning, technology and local entrepreneurship.

The workstation is the economic engine.

The node is the mission.

---

## Year One Goals

- Renovate one room in Cow Lane.
- Create two quiet workstations.
- Install reliable internet and cooling.
- Test remote digital work (Upwork and similar platforms).
- Document every step.
- Build a reproducible model for future nodes.

---

## Long-term Vision

Income generated in the workstation helps finance:

- maker space
- AI access
- gaming evenings
- 3D printing
- education
- homework support
- local entrepreneurship
- future Edge nodes

# Success Criteria

The first year is successful if:

- One quiet workstation is operational.
- Two people can work there every weekday.
- Stable internet and cooling are maintained.
- Income begins covering operating costs.
- The entire process is documented.

The first node should become a reference implementation for future Solvinter Edge locations.
