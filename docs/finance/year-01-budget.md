# Year 1 Budget — Accra Node 0.1

## Purpose

The first year is focused on building one operational Solvinter Edge node in Accra.

The node contains two layers:

1. A small income-generating workstation room.
2. A wider community and learning node.

The workstation room is not the final vision. It is the economic engine that helps finance the node.

## Year 1 Goal

Renovate 1–2 rooms in Cow Lane, Accra, for 2–4 digital workstations.

Initial target:

- 2 operational workstations.
- Stable internet.
- Air-conditioned workroom.
- Basic acoustic treatment.
- Documented operations.
- First remote digital work income.

## Revenue Model

The first revenue model is human digital work.

Possible work categories:

- Upwork-like freelance tasks
- data annotation
- AI-assisted tasks
- remote support
- research
- transcription
- translation
- basic design
- software testing
- bookkeeping support
- small business planning support

GPU rendering, Storj, storage and compute are secondary experiments in year 1.

## Year 1 Success Criteria

The project succeeds in year 1 if:

- the room is renovated
- 2 workstations are operational
- at least one person is trained
- first remote-work income is generated
- monthly costs are understood
- the process is documented
- the next version of the model becomes clearer
