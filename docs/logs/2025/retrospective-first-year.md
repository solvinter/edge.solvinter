# First Year Retrospective

Status: draft outline

## Purpose

Document the first year of Solvinter as it moved from thinking and experimentation toward a practical Cow Lane node.

## Outline

- Several months where the main available resources were time, a computer and internet access.
- Early Solvinter in Sweden as a light therapy and meditation room idea.
- Ghana as a possible solar-powered cooling and workspace environment.
- Mining as the first economic hypothesis.
- Rendering and decentralized compute as later hypotheses.
- Starlink as a practical connectivity layer.
- Hardware acquired or tested: GPUs, Raspberry Pis, server, solar panel, MPPT, batteries and related infrastructure.
- Shift from pure compute toward human-centered AI work.
- Cow Lane as the first practical node.

## Questions

- Which parts created real learning?
- Which hypotheses should be archived as no longer primary?
- What equipment is still useful for Cow Lane?
- How did the economic model change over time?
