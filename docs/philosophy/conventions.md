# Conventions

## Observation

A convention allows a short expression to represent a much larger amount of shared structure.

## Education

Education may not primarily increase factual knowledge.

It may instead increase the number and richness of conventions a person can use.

## Writing

Readers only understand high-density expressions if the required conventions have first been established.

The task of the essayist is often to build those conventions before relying on them.

## Solvinter

The command `sol` is an example of a convention.

A short expression replaces an entire operational workflow.
